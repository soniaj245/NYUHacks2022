FaceTune and Social Media

The Exploration of Gender Norms in Social Media

With the rise of social media, our online presence impacts much of our daily lives, whether it is our careers, our social lives, or anything in between. However, the gender norms that are present in our physical lives are reflected onto social media and the way those who identify with certain genders react to it. Women on average spend much more time and money in curating their social media pages to showcase their lives and physical appearance. Men on the otherhand are often praised for not using social media at all, let alone allocating any resources to developing their online presence. In this project, we'd like to show the resources, whether it be time or money, women put into adjusting their online presence to meet the gender norms put onto them.
